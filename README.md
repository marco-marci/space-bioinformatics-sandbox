# 🪐space-bioinformatics-sandbox
This is my personal laboratory for computational space biology; it contains some projects and simulations I will develop during my Bioinformatics studies at the University of Verona.

## 🎯Main objective
My goal is to understand how bioinformatics can help space research focusing on the health of astronauts and omics data and get closer to the world of research that I am passionate about.

## 💻Tech Stack
* **Language:** C
* **Environment:** Unix/Linux
